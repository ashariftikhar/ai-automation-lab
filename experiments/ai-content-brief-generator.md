# AI Content Brief Generator

## 🎯 Objective

Design an AI-powered system that generates structured content briefs for SEO-focused blog articles.

---

## 🧠 Concept

Input:
- Target keyword
- Audience type
- Search intent

AI Output:
- Article structure
- Headings (H1, H2, H3)
- Content angle
- FAQs
- Internal linking suggestions
- CTA strategy
